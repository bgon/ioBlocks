Whether you're an teacher stirring the next generation of problem-solvers, a parent modeling STEM skills with your children or a Designer mocking-up your next awesomeness, we'll help you create the next generation of Internet connected Things.

Modular

From USB powerbanks to wifi and html: No proprietary/fancy/cloudy connector protocol.

Physical

Physical software! Available for kids from 4 years. Learn coding concepts without annoying syntax errors: away from the screen!

Easy

Compatible with most construction toys sets (Oxford®, Lego®, mega-blocks®, meccano®..etc). You don't need to reinvent the wheel.
